ABOUT CLOUD CONSULTING

Cloud Consulting is a firm that specializes in custom-made web design services. We utilize the use of freelancers to complete our client contracts. For certain projects or if requested by our clients we also provide in-house web development. Whether you are an individual or small business we take pride in our ability to help you achieve your goals by designing well-structured and fully functional websites that will boost your presence online. We are a freelancing service with our web designer as project manager. We also have other core members of our staff that facilitate the exchange of information and coordination between our client and designer. We specialize in working with small businesses and individuals. As our team is small and, aside from our own in-house lead designer, comprised mostly of freelance designers, we usually do not take on contracts with larger firms or projects that require multiple designers.

Platform

The platforms in which we work with to develop websites for clients include wordpress, wix, magento and joomla. We also use various codes including html, css, javascript, php to build the website. Overall we use the latest tools and techniques that are in demand or expected of within the current digital environment to build a well functional and appropriate website.

Passion

We here at Cloud Consulting feel that passion is the most important and fundamental characteristic of producing quality work. As such we take pride and in the work we do and we love what we do. When we accept a contract we try to fulfill the requirements with the utmost respect for our clients and ensure that our fiduciary duty is well above par.

Cost

It varies from client to client. If you are looking for something simple then it will be much less naturally, usually around $150+ but something that requires much more functionality such as SEO and e-commerce then it will cost much more. Other things such as revisions and number of domains will also affect the price. We have a general summary of the prices in our services section.

Support

If you are a client and you need any assistance then do not be afraid to contact us anytime. Whether by e-mail, phone, or in person. You can also reach us at our social media platforms if you desire. Any questions or concerns that you may have even if they are minuscule we would be happy to clear them up. Or if you are just thinking about making a website you can contact us and we can set up a consultation.
